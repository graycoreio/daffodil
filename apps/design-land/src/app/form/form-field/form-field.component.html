Form Field

<daff-form-field> is a wrapping component for form control elements. It is used to style certain form controls (<input>'s, <select>'s, and <textarea>'s) that would otherwise be impossible to style with normal css. It also organizes error messages and hints alongside their associated form controls.

Prefix and Suffix

Additional text or button can be placed before and after a form control by using the daffPrefix and daffSuffix attributes.

Label

daffFormLabel is applied to the <label> element. It is used to provide users with a short hint that assists with data entry. For accessibility considerations, it should always be used in conjuction with form elements within a <daff-form-field>. An error will occur if daffFormLabel is not defined.
