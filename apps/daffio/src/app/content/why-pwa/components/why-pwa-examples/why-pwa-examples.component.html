Who's building PWAs?

Brands are increasingly utilizing Progressive Web Apps to bridge the gap between native apps and websites to create better user experiences.

Uber

Uber built their PWA to minimize the client footprint while maintaining the rich experience of the native app.

Starbucks

The Starbucks PWA makes their service universally accessible to both established and emerging markets.

Lancôme

Lancôme's PWA aimed to drive traffic and re-engagement, which ultimately saw a 17% increase in conversions.
